About Us

Precious C. Enoch, Esq. Principal Partner

Enoch and Enoch Legal is a dynamic corporate and compliance law firm established in 2024 with a vision to provide high-quality legal services tailored for businesses navigating today's complex legal and regulatory environment.

Though a young firm, we are committed to excellence, integrity, and client-centric solutions. Our legal practice focuses on corporate advisory, regulatory compliance, commercial transactions, and insolvency practice.

We pride ourselves on building long-term, trusted relationships with our clients through practical legal advice, proactive risk mitigation, and strategic legal support.
