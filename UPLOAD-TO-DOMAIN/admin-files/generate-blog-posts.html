📝 Blog Post Generator

Generate individual blog post HTML files from your blog data

How to Use:

  1. Add your blog posts to blog-data.js file
  2. Click "Generate Blog Posts" button below
  3. Copy the generated HTML for each post
  4. Save each as blog-post-[ID].html in your website folder
  5. Your blog posts will now be accessible on the website!
