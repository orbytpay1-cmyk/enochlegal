About Us

Precious C. Enoch, Esq. Principal Partner

Enoch and Enoch Legal is a dynamic corporate and compliance law firm established in 2024 with a vision to provide high-quality legal services tailored for businesses navigating today's complex legal and regulatory environment.

Though a young firm, we are committed to excellence, integrity, and client-centric solutions. Our legal practice focuses on corporate advisory, regulatory compliance, commercial transactions, and insolvency practice.

We pride ourselves on building long-term, trusted relationships with our clients through practical legal advice, proactive risk mitigation, and strategic legal support.

Precious C. Enoch, Esq.

Principal Partner

Precious C. Enoch, Esq. is the Principal Partner of Enoch & Enoch Legal, a firm structured to deliver clear, practical, and commercially sound legal solutions. Her practice spans commercial law, insolvency, mediation, debt recovery, and regulatory compliance, with a focus on protecting value, managing risk, and resolving disputes efficiently.

At Enoch & Enoch Legal, she advises individuals and businesses on corporate governance, contract structuring and enforcement, compliance obligations, and dispute resolution. Her approach reflects the firm's commitment to professionalism, precision, and responsiveness, ensuring that legal advice is aligned with clients' objectives and operational realities.

Through strategic documentation, effective negotiation, and structured dispute resolution, Precious leads the firm in providing dependable legal support that promotes certainty, compliance, and sustainable outcomes for clients.
