## 🚀 How to Use

### Publishing a Blog Post:

1. Go to admin dashboard
2. Login with credentials
3. Click "New Post" tab
4. Fill in all fields
5. **Drag & drop** an image or click the upload area
6. Preview your image
7. Click "Publish Post"
8. Post appears immediately on the live site!

### Managing Posts:

1. Click "View Articles" tab
2. Click any category to expand
3. See all posts with thumbnails
4. Click "Delete" to remove a post
